Insights & Next Steps

Priority actions · effort vs impact · growth roadmap

Immediate

Add App Store Link to YouTube Descriptions

864 organic viewers currently have no path to download. Add the App Store URL to all 10 video descriptions now — free, 5-minute fix with potential 17–43 additional downloads at $0 cost.

⏱ 5 min effort 💰 $0 cost 📈 High impact
High Priority

Fix YouTube Ad Deep Link Tracking

$8.41 was spent on YouTube Paid with zero install attribution. Add the App Store URL with UTM parameters (utm_source=youtube&utm_medium=paid) to the ad landing page — unlocks true CPI measurement.

⏱ 1 hr effort 💰 $0 cost 🎯 Tracking fix
High Priority

Scale Apple Ads Converting Keywords

"peak flow" delivered a $0.05 CPI — best-in-class performance. "health insights" and "health monitoring" each produced 1 confirmed install. Double budget on asthma-specific terms and pause broad terms like "ai" ($2.65, 0 installs).

⏱ 30 min effort 💰 Budget increase 🚀 Immediate ROI
Medium Priority

Connect Firebase to BigQuery

Currently flying blind on in-app behaviour. BigQuery integration unlocks: which features drive retention, when users churn, what triggers the power user's 25-session days, and whether onboarding is completing.

⏱ 1 day dev 💰 $0 cost 🔬 Analytics unlock
Medium Priority

Connect Meta Ads Data Export

Meta Ads performance is a complete blind spot. Export from Meta Ads Manager → Reports to populate this dashboard. Cannot optimise Meta spend without knowing reach, CPM, CTR, and estimated installs.

⏱ Export CSV 💰 $0 cost 📊 Data visibility
Medium Priority

Improve App Store Icon & Screenshots

3.0% impression-to-tap rate means 97% of users scroll past the app in search results. A/B test a new icon and a results-focused first screenshot (e.g. "Breathe Easier in 7 Days"). Industry leaders hit 4–6% tap rate.

⏱ Design work 💰 Design cost 📱 ASO impact
